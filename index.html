Merrimack Valley Robotics (MVR)

Our parent organization is MVR, an IRS-approved 501(c)(3) public charity founded to financially enable FRC Team 5962. MVR now also supports 30+ FIRST LEGO League Jr. and several FIRST LEGO League teams for K-4 and middle school students, respectively.
